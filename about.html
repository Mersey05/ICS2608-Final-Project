OUR STORY

WHY MUSHROOM COFFEE?

At Calle Mush, we’re always looking to bring something special to the coffee scene. With so many coffee shops and pop-up coffee bar opening lately, we wanted to offer something truly unique, something that stands out in flavor and experience. That's when we had the idea to use mushrooms in our coffee! Though it’s still a rare find in many cafes, mushroom coffee intrigued us with its rich taste and potential health benefits. It’s a blend that’s both surprising and fulfilling—giving you all the comfort of coffee with a fun twist.

Our Values:

What Drives Calle Mush?

INNOVATION

We’re passionate about creativity and exploring unique flavors. With specialty drinks like our mushroom coffee, we aim to deliver a taste that’s refreshingly different from the usual.

HEALTH & SAFETY

Cleanliness and safety are always top priorities at Calle Mush. We work hard to ensure a healthy, welcoming space for both our customers and our team, so every visit feels as good as it tastes.

CUSTOMER-CENTRIC SERVICE

Our team is trained to provide you exceptional customer service. From friendly greetings to knowledgeable recommendations, we’re here to give you an experience as memorable as our coffee.

OUR JOURNEY

OVERCOMING CHALLENGES

Starting something new is always a challenge, and finding the right ingredients was no different. Finding suppliers for high-quality mushrooms and coffee is difficult—especially since mushroom coffee is still quite uncommon. But with determination, we found suppliers that provide affordable options and matched our commitment to quality.

WHAT SETS US APART

Each cup we serve is a little taste of adventure, intended to give you something different from the usual coffee experience. From our specialty drinks to our friendly service, Calle Mush is here to make your coffee breaks a bit more special!

"MORE THAN JUST A COFFEE"

While mushroom coffee may sound unusual, it has its perks! Companies that produce mushroom coffee assert that it can help reduce stress and support immune health—though research is still growing. What we know for sure is that it’s packed with unique compounds that add a new layer of flavor and richness to your coffee. Whether you’re here for the taste or the possible health benefits, mushroom coffee is a delicious way to shake up your coffee routine!
